About Me

I design and build seamless digital experiences. As a UI/UX Designer with a background in creative front-end development, I've spent over 5 years crafting impactful products for major organizations like Volkswagen, Santander, and Pearson. My unique blend of skills in Figma, Lottie, Advanced CSS, and JavaScript (React, Vue, and Angular) allows me to bridge the gap between design and engineering, ensuring every solution is both beautiful and functional. I specialize in turning complex user needs into elegant solutions across industries like Education, Healthcare, and Social Media. Off the clock, I enjoy inline skating and DIY projects.

I am a product-focused software engineer who thrives on building impactful digital experiences. My journey started with a deep-seated desire to help creators monetize their work easily and reliably.

As a founding member of a social platform for content creators, I was responsible for both product design and front-end development for our MVP. I conducted interviews, rapid prototyping, and iterative development to validate our ideas and attract our first users. My core mission was to address the major pain points of Brazilian creators, particularly around financial management and faster payouts.

Through user research and a hands-on development approach, I helped shape the platform from its earliest stage, focusing on creating a seamless and engaging experience for both creators and their audiences. My work involved identifying opportunities to differentiate in a competitive market and executing on viable growth strategies to expand our user base.
